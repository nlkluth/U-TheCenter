Founded in 2012, U The Center strives to create educational tools that allow students to build professional profiles through online collaboration.

Monte Eady

Founder

Monte is a recent graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee with a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design. He has been developing a system that he hopes will change the way students communicate amongst each other. Monte wants to make life on campus easier for all students by providing a tool where they can connect with each other. Whether it is for a school project, playing in a band, or getting a photographer for your wedding, the resources are on your campus. Monte just want to make finding them easy.

Kierston Ghaznavi

Art Director

Kierston Ghaznavi has always had an interest in art and illustration. As a child she would get in trouble in school for drawing all over her homework. She knew her future career would take her down a creative path. Kierston received her BFA in Graphic Design at the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee in May 2012. She is most interested in pen and vector illustrations as well as typography and branding. She was a part of the design team responsible for the branding and visual look of the Design Entrepreneur Showcase for Graphic Design II last semester. In the future, Kierston hopes to contribute to the effort of raising awareness about the need for organ and tissue donors in Wisconsin, hopefully working in design locally.

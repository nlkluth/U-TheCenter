The Center

Everyone is an Innovator, Researcher, Resource.

Our platform offers students a new way to connect and engage campus-wide.

It is important to create a product and service that benefits not only the students, but also everyone within the campus community. It is our goal to create a tool of access and research within the campus community. We want to reach the students that are supporters of success and students that want to succeed. U The Center is a company that will be beneficial to any educational institution that focuses on the well-being and preparation oftheir students and their success.

Collaboration

Connection

Students create connections with others to expand research on projects, collaborate on new opportunities.

Evolving Portfolio

Students can create online portfolios to showcase projects and works in progress as well as receive feedback and critiques to reach ideal resolutions.

Community

U TheCenter gives students an online community to collaborate on projects, network and create new connections, access research opportunities, and a source for advice from faculty and peers.

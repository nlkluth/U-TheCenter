Monte Eady

Founder

Monte is a recent graduate of the University of Wisconsin-Milwaukee with a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design. He has been developing a system that he hopes will change the way students communicate amongst each other. Monte wants to make life on campus easier for all students by providing a tool where they can connect with each other.
